# 🎬 Inception

**Director:** *Christopher Nolan*  
**Release Year:** 2010  
**Genre:** Science Fiction / Thriller  

---

## 🧠 Plot Summary
A skilled thief, **Dom Cobb**, steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state.  
He’s offered a chance to **erase his criminal record** if he can perform the impossible — *inception*, planting an idea into someone’s mind.

---

### 🌟 Main Cast
- Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb  
- Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur  
- Ellen Page as Ariadne  
- Tom Hardy as Eames  

---

> “You mustn’t be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling.” — *Eames*
